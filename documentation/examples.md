# Viva Examples and Simulated Flows
> This document is generated by `scripts/generate_examples_md.py`.

All flows below were produced using `generateFlowEngine(...)`.

**Simple one-shot usage:**

```python
engine = generateFlowEngine(source, start_year=..., horizon_years=...)
flows = engine.drawFlows(seed=...)
```

**For controlled sampling or statistics (recommended):**

```python
engine = generateFlowEngine(...)
engine.params              # declared random variables
distribs = engine.param_distribs
flows = engine.getFlows(**realizations)   # or engine.getFlows(dict)
```

See the main README for the full `FlowEngine` API.
Dates are January 1 of each year (MVP).

# Full Syntax Coverage Example
# Demonstrates many Viva syntactic features not (fully) covered in the basic examples.
# Goal: exercise parser + interpreter for completeness.

event: risk_event, probability 15% per year  # probability-only event

# Flows demonstrating amounts
flow: initial_capital, 2.5 million, upon launch   # decimal + word unit
flow: operating_budget, -50000 per year, upon launch, for 3 years   # plain number + for
flow: revenue_share, 10000 annually, after 2 years   # after standalone + annually keyword on amount
flow: founder_salary, 120k per year, upon launch, for 10 years

# From / to / until compact forms + birth/death refs
flow: founder_vesting, 50000 per year, from Founder.birth, for 4 years   # from .birth
flow: exit_bonus, 1 million, from year 2026   # one-time with from year (no duration => no period needed)
flow: growth_phase, 30000 per year, from year 2026 until year 2030
flow: bridge_loan, 25000 per year, from year 3, for 2 years   # small relative year

# Until variants (total with word, year)
flow: r_and_d, -8000 per month, upon launch, until 200 thousand total   # word total + per month
flow: legacy_payout, 500k, upon death_payout_trigger   # one-time with life_ref event trigger

# Complex flow modifiers
flow: retirement_income, 80000 per year, upon founder_retirement, starting at age 60, for life
flow: post_acquisition, 100k per year, upon acquisition, after 2 years, for 5 years

# One-time and recurring without upon (implicit from start_year)
flow: office_rent, -12000 per year
flow: one_time_setup, -15000, upon launch
